About Our Organization

Improve and sustain the environmental
protection for economic empowerment.

Nurture Foundation (NuF) is a locally registered organization in Rwanda, established in 2022 with a mission to improve and sustain environmental protection while empowering vulnerable sections of society, particularly youth and women. Through strategic interventions and impactful programs, NuF bridges the gap between environmental sustainability and economic empowerment, ensuring that vulnerable communities can thrive in harmony with nature.

  • Our Mission

    To link Women and Youth to economic opportunities sustained by implementing interventions that are environmentally friendly and through recreational art programs to develop their potentials.

  • Our Vision

    To create an enabling environment to achieve a just and sustainable future by the vulnerable sections of society, youth and women.

Introducing Our Ecoplan

Targeted and skilling programs

NuF works alongside other organizations to train and equip communities with knowledge to improve their livelihoods from poverty to economic prosperity particularly women who shoulder the impacts of environmental degradation. Youth are equipped with knowledge on conservation.

Community outreach programs

NuF works with communities at its core reaching them with diversified messages relating to conservation efforts geared at development and protection of the environment, fostering unity through recreational efforts and opportunities for growth and support. From organizing local events to spearheading educational programs, NuF, efforts create sustainability pathways owned by communities.

Gender-environment Nexus

The differentiated impacts of climate change on men and women are closely linked to factors leading to gender inequalities and violence. Impacts such as division of labor, domestic responsibilities, control and access to resources result from scarcity thereof. When women have rights to productive economic resources such as land, natural resources and biodiversity, access to food, energy, water and sanitation, their well-being steer equalities that lead to wholesome robust health for all.

Sustainable environmental protection interventions

NuF is concerned with the ability to save our environment, ensures the relationship of humans with the environment are sustainable creating a harmonious environment for people to thrive. NuF deals with a restorative approach to improve our very existence while simultaneously conserving vital resources that may take hundreds (or even thousands) of years to restore once they have become depleted.

Youth education through recreation activities

NuF seeks to create cohesion among communities to create social relations leading to productive interactions involuntary interpersonal relationships lead to improvement of livelihoods through sports and other young people focused activities.

Partnerships and Collaboration

NuF is a partnership organization. It works alongside the efforts of the GoR and other development partners to realize its goals impacting the lives of the vulnerable impacting communities for a sustained future leaving a planet able to support lives for generations to come.

Our approach

The organization recognizes that when communities, particularly youth and women, are empowered with the tools and opportunities to succeed, they become agents of change capable of steering sustainable development.

  • Women Empowerment and Youth Education

    Addressing the disproportionate impact of environmental degradation on women and youth, NuF offers training and resources to lift them from poverty to economic prosperity.

  • Community-Led Sustainability Pathways

    Through participatory approaches, NuF ensures communities take ownership of their development and conservation efforts.

  • Sports and Youth Engagement

    Organized sports and community-driven activities enhance interpersonal relationships, improve livelihoods, and encourage active participation in conservation efforts.
